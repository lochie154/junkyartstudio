<p>
  Our late summer show at <a href="https://phasespace.nyc/" rel="noopener" target="_blank">Phase Space</a> was on Saturday, August 27! I had an excellent time performing as part of REALITY ORP, a new collaborative project with <a href="https://andreijaycreativecoding.com/" rel="noopener" target="_blank">Andrei Jay</a>. I performed live with liquids for the first time, and combined that with analog video processing.
